HTML Copycat Exercise

What's one of your favourite websites to visit? Imitation is the highest form of flattery, so why dont you try to imitate it?

  1. Open up the webpage and keep it open as reference.
  2. Create a basic webpage wth that webpages content (or at least a small subset of it, like a blog post if it's a blog).
  3. Set up the layout for the webpage using a framework like Twitter Bootstrap.
  4. Change the text and background color to match.
  5. Figure out if you need a custom font to match their font.
  6. Keep going until it looks close.

All done? (or close enough)? Share what you made with everyone!
